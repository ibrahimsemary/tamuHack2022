Campus tour

A guided walking tour of central campus that includes information about the history and traditions of Texas A&M with stops including Kyle Field, the Quad, and Academic Plaza. You may also add a Bonfire Memorial and/or Memorial Student Center tour to your campus tour for a full Aggie experience. (1 hour)

MSC tour

Come walk through the living memorial and learn about the history and symbolism of the building while catching a glimpse of student life, including the "living room of Texas A&M" with The Flag Room, the university bookstore, art galleries and more. (30 minutes)

Bonfire tour

The Bonfire Memorial embodies the history and tradition of Texas A&M and is dedicated to those involved in the tragic collapse of the 1999 Bonfire. Experience the history and tradition of Aggie Bonfire with a walkthrough of the memorial by a trained guide. (30 minutes)
